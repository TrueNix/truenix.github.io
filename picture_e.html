English Bulldogs: Wrinkled Charms

The English Bulldog is a breed known for its distinctive wrinkled face, stocky build, and surprisingly gentle demeanor. Despite their somewhat tough appearance, these dogs are renowned for being **affectionate, loyal**, and excellent companions.

Appearance

English Bulldogs are medium-sized dogs with a muscular frame. They typically weigh between 40-50 pounds and stand about 14-15 inches tall. Their most recognizable features include:

  • Wrinkled Face: Deep folds of skin on their forehead and muzzle.
  • Pushed-in Nose: A very short snout, which can sometimes lead to breathing difficulties.
  • Underbite: Their lower jaw often protrudes beyond the upper jaw.
  • Broad Shoulders: Giving them a powerful and sturdy look.
  • Short, Fine Coat: Usually in colors like brindle, white, fawn, or piebald.
