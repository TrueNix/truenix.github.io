Coastal Coffee: A Short Guide

Quick tips for a clean, consistent cup when you’re by the sea.

Brewing Tips

  • Use a slightly finer grind; shorten bloom by 10–15s.
  • If tap water is chalky, use filtered or bottled water (~75–150 ppm as CaCO3).
  • Rinse the filter and preheat the brewer; target total brew time of 2:45–3:15.

Method

  1. Rinse filter and warm your vessel.
  2. Bloom with twice the dose in grams for ~20s near the coast.
  3. Pour in slow circles to keep the bed flat; finish within 2:45–3:15.
