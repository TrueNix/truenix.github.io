Clickjacking Attack Simulation

The grey box below contains a decoy button and a semi-transparent iframe.

Your goal is to align the target button from the iframe (e.g., "Confirm Action") directly over the "Click for Prize!" button by adjusting the `top` and `left` values in the CSS for `#decoy-button`.

Once aligned, change the iframe's `opacity` to `0` to make it fully invisible and perform the attack.

How it Works

  1. The page loads the vulnerable URL (`/c/123123123`) in a transparent iframe.
  2. Since you are logged into `localhost`, the iframe loads your authenticated session.
  3. The iframe is placed on top of the decoy button.
  4. When you click the decoy, you are actually clicking the button on the legitimate, but hidden, page.
  5. This triggers the action on the vulnerable page, which is to navigate to `/?q=123123`.
