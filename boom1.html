        ## PREAMBLE
        This document codifies the principles governing the interaction between OmniCorp personnel and the corporate digital ecosystem.         ## SECTION 1: ASSET CLASSIFICATION
        
        1.1: TANGIBLE DIGITAL ASSETS (TDAs): Company-issued laptops, peripherals, mobile devices.
        
        1.2: INTANGIBLE DIGITAL ASSETS (IDAs):
            1.2.1: Tier 1 (Proprietary): Source code, financial projections, the secret formula for the cafeteria's chili.
            1.2.2: Tier 2 (Operational): Internal emails, project management boards, chat logs.
            1.2.3: Tier 3 (Ephemeral): Browser cache, temporary files, unsent angry drafts.
        
        ## SECTION 12: BROWSER AND NETWORK USAGE (EXCERPT)
        
        12.5: HISTORY AND TELEMETRY: All browsing activity is logged for security and diagnostic purposes. The logs are analyzed by our proprietary AI, "The Panopticon," which flags deviations from established productivity baselines.
        
        ## SECTION 28: DATA EXFILTRATION (EXCERPT)
        
        28.1: DEFINITION: Exfiltration is the unauthorized transfer of IDAs outside the corporate network. This includes, but is not limited to: copying files to personal drives, emailing documents to a personal account, or embedding proprietary data within the URL parameters of a public-facing website.
